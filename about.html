Polaris Parks was officially launched in January 2008, as the first initiative of its kind in Egypt, both as a private industrial park.

Capablities

Polaris Parks is the place for good environment and successful production.

01
WHY ?

Polaris Parks works for the development of the country together with its industry, in order to adopt and compete in the global economy.

02
HOW ?

Polaris Parks prepare the necessary environment for the industry while aiding investors through difficulties of start-up period and moving into operation phases.

03
WHAT ?

In order to do this Polaris Parks develops unutilized areas into industrial compounds with finest infrastructure networks, offering value added facilities and providing professional management systems.

04
when ?

We are racing against time to prepare the development environment that keeps pace with the times by following the latest technological standards in our field.

Industrial Parks are areas with necessary infrastructure dedicated to attracting different types of investors to start or move their business to be inside the place.

Industrial Parks aim to ensure that industrial activities are carried out in inappropriate places as well as the other activities and services. To be able to attract new businesses by providing an integrated infrastructure in one location. To set aside industrial uses from urban areas to try to reduce the environmental and social impact of the industrial uses & to provide for localized environmental controls that are specific to the needs of an industrial area.

EGYPT

Officially the Arab Republic of Egypt, is a transcontinental country spanning the northeast corner of Africa and southwest corner of Asia by a bridge formed by the Sinai Peninsula. Egypt is a Mediterranean country bordered by the Gaza Strip to the northeast, the Gulf of Aqaba to the east, the Red Sea to the east and south, Sudan to the south, and Libya to the west. Across the Gulf of Aqaba lies Jordan, and across from the Sinai Peninsula lies Saudi Arabia, although Jordan and Saudi Arabia do not share a land border with Egypt. It is the world’s only contiguous Afrasian nation.
